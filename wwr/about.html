History

Founded in 2012 by a group of river enthusiasts and outdoor guides, Canary Rafting Company began as a small, local outfit offering weekend rafting trips on the Upper Clearwater River. What started with a single raft and a big dream quickly grew into a full-service adventure company known for safety, skill, and the sheer joy of the ride. Over the years, we’ve introduced thousands of guests to the rush of whitewater, expanded our offerings to include multi-day expeditions, and remained committed to protecting the wild places we love. Today, we’re proud to be one of the region’s most trusted names in river adventure.
